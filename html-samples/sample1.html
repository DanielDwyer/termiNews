How Donald Trump really got rich.

THE TRUMP INHERITANCE

4 Ways Fred Trump Made Donald Trump and His Siblings Rich

  • In Donald Trump’s version of how he got rich, he was the master dealmaker who parlayed a $1 million loan from his father into a $10 billion empire.   
  • But The Times’s investigation found that the president’s father created scores of revenue streams for his son.

11 Takeaways From The Times’s Investigation Into Trump’s Wealth

Based on a trove of confidential financial records, the Times report offers the first comprehensive look at the inherited fortune and tax dodges that guaranteed Donald Trump a gilded life.

Supreme Court Nominee

F.B.I. to Complete Inquiry Wednesday With Vote Coming This Week

  • Once its investigation into Judge Brett M. Kavanaugh is completed, the F.B.I. will send reports about its interviews to the Senate.
  • Senator Mitch McConnell, the majority leader, would not say whether this week’s vote would be a final vote or a procedural one.

Trump Mocks the Woman Accusing Kavanaugh of Sexual Assault

At a rally in Mississippi, President Trump mimicked Christine Blasey Ford’s testimony last week. The crowd laughed and cheered.

“We’re loud, obnoxious drunks,” Brett Kavanaugh wrote of his high school clique in 1983.

Tsunami's Aftermath

‘A Mass Burial Every Day’: 1,200 Dead, and Counting, in Indonesia

  • Friday’s earthquake and tsunami in Indonesia killed more than 1,200 people and destroyed thousands of homes.
  • But officials say many more days of hardship remain before the extent of the loss is known.

See before-and-after photos of the destruction.

What Went Wrong With Indonesia’s Tsunami Early Warning System

None of the country’s detection systems were able to adequately predict the scale of the tsunami.

‘Better to Drown’: A Greek Refugee Camp’s Epidemic of Misery

  • Trauma, psychosis and suicide attempts have become common occurrences at Moria, a Greek island camp, where aid groups warn of a mental health crisis.
  • The conditions have fueled accusations that the camp has been left to fester in order to deter migration.

Social Media

Use Facebook to Log In Elsewhere? Hack May Have Breached Your Other Accounts, Too

  • Ten years ago, the social network introduced a password system that connected it to a broad swath of the internet. Now the downside is evident.
  • Companies that allow customers to log in with Facebook Connect are scrambling to figure out whether their own user accounts have been compromised.

Our tech columnist is going to quit using Facebook to log in to other sites. He thinks you should do the same.
